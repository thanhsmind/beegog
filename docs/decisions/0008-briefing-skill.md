## Rationale

- **The information mostly exists; the human-grade consolidation does not.** Mapping Antigravity's 12 sections against bee's chain: ~8 are already produced (terse, scattered), and only four are genuine gaps â€” one readable document, a Technical Design narrative, a Rollback plan, and a visible Review Status. The skill closes the consolidation gap without duplicating the planning that fills the other eight.
- **Why not extend an existing skill.** `bee-planning` is already the heaviest skill and its outputs are agent-shaped *by design*; folding a human-audience artifact into it blurs both audiences and makes the renderer un-callable on demand. `bee-scribing` owns durable, tech-*agnostic* state (`docs/specs/`) under the opposite rule â€” the brief is per-feature, tech-*specific*, and lives in history. The Gate Presentation Contract alone fixes only the chat layer, which evaporates; the gap is precisely the durable middle layer.
- **RED baseline reshaped the skill from enforcement to procedure.** Six pressure scenarios (design-smuggling, ceremony bloat, validation fabrication, hand-editing the render, invention-to-fill-template, unhinted lane-scaling) were run without the skill. **All six passed** â€” the Fable/Opus-tier agent derived source-of-truth, projection, evidence-honesty, no-invention, and lane-scaling from first principles. The honest reading (consistent with bee's "personas are thin lens contracts, not knowledge dumps" standard): heavy anti-rationalization machinery here would be bloat the model skips. The skill's real value is a **repeatable render procedure + reliable triggering + the two new authored sections**, with the negations kept as a short Red Flags list â€” cheap insurance for weaker runtimes (Codex, cheap worker tiers). This makes `bee-briefing` a thin workflow-stage skill in the `bee-scribing` mold, not a discipline skill in the `bee-writing-skills` mold.

## Alternatives considered

- **Bigger `plan.md` / a template section inside planning.** Rejected: mixes the agent audience (executable cells) with the human audience (reviewable narrative) in one artifact, and the renderer can't be re-invoked on demand ("regenerate the plan doc").

- **Gate Presentation Contract only (no artifact).** Rejected: the contract already governs the chat layer; the owner's pain is precisely that chat is ephemeral and the linked reports are machine-layer. The durable human document is the missing piece.
